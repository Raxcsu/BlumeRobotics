Who we are?


We are a startup focused on helping aquaculture companies to accelerate the monitoring and maintenance of their fishnets where is required scuba divers. As a first step we are focusing on the salmon industry in Chile.


WHAT WE SOLVE?


We specialize in solving the problems faced by salmon companies, specifically the maintenance of networks for salmon farming.

HOW DO WE DO IT?


We use the robots developed by us to inspect and monitor the networks applying artificial intelligence algorithms and with specialized tweezers carry out the corresponding maintenance.

WHY DO WE DO IT?


We want to promote the development of aquaculture in Latin America by creating technology adapted to the conditions that our region faces in order to take advantage of resources in a sustainable way.
